Ledger Mechanics

How the dial and the tension bar are computed — reproducible, not vibes.

Reserve Trust (0–100)

trust = 100 × ( 0.45 × norm(dollar_share_gold_incl) + 0.25 × norm(treasuries_vs_gold_ratio) + 0.20 × (1 − norm(cb_gold_purchases_trailing_4q)) + 0.10 × (1 − norm(gpr_index_12m_avg)) )

Each series is normalised 0–1 against its own historical min/max baseline window (2000–2015 where a real historical feed exists; an editorial estimate otherwise — see the provenance table below). If a term is unavailable on a given day, its weight is dropped and the rest renormalise to sum to 1, rather than the composite going blank.

Geopolitical Tension (0–100)

tension = 100 × ( 0.55 × norm(gpr_monthly) + 0.25 × norm(gdelt_conflict_tone_30d, inverted) + 0.20 × norm(cross_bloc_penalty) )

GPR does most of the work; GDELT's 30-day average tone (inverted — more negative coverage tone raises tension) supplies the between-releases movement; the cross-bloc penalty term raises the floor so tension can't fully calm while the wiring stays structurally split. Same renormalisation rule as trust applies if a term is missing.

Thresholds: Calm <25   Strain <50   Fracture <75   Rupture ≥75
